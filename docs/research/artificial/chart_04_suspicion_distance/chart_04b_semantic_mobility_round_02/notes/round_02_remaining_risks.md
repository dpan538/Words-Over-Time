# Round 02 Remaining Risks

## Main risks

- direct contrast snippets may still be sparse compared with what ideal corpus work would provide
- dictionary evidence is still stronger than corpus evidence for some pairs
- realistic artificial collocations are often modern and product skewed
- human-process exit markers can easily overreach into Chart 5
- visual placement remains interpretive even when evidence is good

## Conceptual safeguards

- fake adjacent does not mean fake
- realistic does not mean real
- natural, genuine, and authentic are not identical
- synthetic is close to artificial without exhausting it
- exit markers should not become core 4B nodes

## Highest-priority follow-up risks

- `artificial vs simulated` still needs better contextual evidence
- `realistic artificial intelligence` is too sparse for confident use
- `artificial companion` and `artificial consciousness` need stronger sourcing before any visual inclusion
