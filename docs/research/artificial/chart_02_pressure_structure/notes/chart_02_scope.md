# Chart 02 Scope

Chart 2 is scoped to semantic pressure around `artificial`.

It follows Chart 1's correction that `artificial` belongs to art, skill, making, contrivance, and technical construction. Chart 2 should not repeat that whole origin story. Instead, it should show the next semantic condition:

```text
artificial begins inside a substitute field,
but becomes pressured by not-natural and not-real meanings,
which creates suspicion.
```
