## 2. What Is Being Semantically Replaced

The reference's original thesis categories are replaced with Chart 2's artificial-pressure model.


The exact words from the reference are not preserved. The formal logic is preserved.

## 6. Why This Layout Suits Chart 2

The reference layout suits Chart 2 because it separates abstract structure from activation. The upper panel can show the semantic model without over-explaining it. The lower panel can reveal concrete evidence without turning the chart into a dense dataset.

It also solves a key semantic problem: `artificial` should not be drawn as the whole field. By placing it as a selected point inside `substitute field`, the diagram shows that the word is located within a field and then pressured by adjacent meanings.

The line-based structure gives `suspicion` the right status. It becomes a tension relation produced by pressure, not a third circle or a simple causal endpoint.
